How We Build Vybe

A week-by-week plan so you always know what's being built and when you can expect to see it.

Week 1 – 2
🎨 Design & Foundation
  • Brand-aligned UI design — Figma mockups shared with you for approval before any code is written
  • Set up codebase, database structure, and hosting environment
  • Design mobile-first homepage, product listing, and product detail pages
  • Create the Admin Panel — simple enough for non-tech users
Week 3 – 4
🛍️ Core Store Features
  • Build product listing with dynamic admin control — add, edit, or remove products anytime
  • Develop cart, wishlist, and customer profile & login system
  • Coupon & offer engine — percentage discounts, flat off, buy-X-get-Y
  • Inventory tracking with low-stock alerts and auto out-of-stock
Week 5
💳 Payments & Checkout
  • Integrate Razorpay/PayU — UPI, cards, netbanking, EMI, and COD
  • Checkout flow — address → payment → confirmation in under 60 seconds
  • Handle success, failure, and partial payment scenarios gracefully
  • Auto-generate and email invoice PDFs to customers on every order
Week 6
🚚 Shipping Automation
  • Full ShipRocket API — auto order creation, AWB, courier assignment, pickup scheduling
  • COD management with remittance tracking dashboard
  • Reverse pickup flow for returns with customer-facing return portal
  • Real-time shipment tracking page for customers
Week 7
💬 Notifications, Blogs & SEO
  • WhatsApp notifications — order placed, shipped, out for delivery, delivered
  • Email notification system with fully branded templates
  • Blog management module — write, schedule, and publish from admin panel
  • Technical SEO across all pages — meta, schema, sitemap, robots.txt
Week 8
📊 Dashboard, QA & Launch
  • Business analytics dashboard — revenue, orders, top products, pending COD, monthly growth
  • Full QA across mobile, tablet, and desktop — real device testing
  • Performance optimisation — page speed and image compression
  • Live deployment, DNS, SSL certificate, and handover training session for your team

Honest Assessment

Is Shopify Right for Omarun?

A transparent breakdown of what you gain and what you give up — so you can make the right call for your business.

✅ Pros
  • ↑
    Quick go-live in 2 weeks — No custom development cycles. Your store is live and selling within 14 days of kickoff.
  • ↑
    Lower upfront cost — ₹45,000 vs ₹2,70,000. A smart entry point to validate sales before committing to a custom platform.
  • ↑
    Proven, stable platform — Shopify handles hosting, security, and uptime. Trusted by 4M+ stores globally.
  • ↑
    Easy product management — Your team can manage listings without any training or technical knowledge.
  • ↑
    App ecosystem — Thousands of plug-and-play apps for reviews, upsells, email, and more.
⚠️ Cons
  • ↓
    Transaction fee on every 3rd party payment (1–2%) — On ₹10L/month revenue, that is ₹10,000–₹20,000 lost every single month to Shopify. On Vybe it is ₹0.
  • ↓
    Limited dashboard & business reports — No COD remittance tracking, no product-wise profit view, no custom business metrics unless you pay for expensive third-party apps.
  • ↓
    Scaling gets expensive — Basic plan (₹2,000/mo) limits product listings. The Grow plan hits ₹8,000/month — that is ₹96,000/year just for the platform.
  • ↓
    You do not own the platform — Shopify can change pricing or remove features. With Vybe, you own 100% of the code permanently.
  • ↓
    No custom brand storytelling — Shopify themes are generic. Building immersive Ayurvedic product pages requires expensive custom theme development.
🧠
Our Honest Verdict

Shopify makes sense if Omarun wants to test the market in 2 weeks with a low upfront investment. But here is the real math: the 1–2% transaction fee alone at ₹10L/month adds up to ₹1,20,000–₹2,40,000 lost per year — money going to Shopify, not your business. Add ₹96,000/year for the Grow plan and you have spent more than the cost of Vybe within 18 months, with no ownership, no custom dashboard, and no brand differentiation.

Our recommendation: If budget is the only constraint today, start Shopify — but plan to migrate to Vybe within 12–18 months. Or invest in Vybe now and own your platform forever.
